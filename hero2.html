Patrick Benard Emojong.

Patrick is our vice president and director of quality control and compliance. He has more than 10years working experience in the life science that includes diagnostic, research laboratories and clinical research organizations. Mr Patrick has a range of experience working with KEMRI DLSP with a foundation in serology, molecular biology and quality assurance in laboratories.

He worked as a member of the team which prepared the laboratory for reaccreditation and got reaccredited. Patrick is an expert in biosafety and biosecurity training and assessment. Patrick began a new global role as a GCLP Auditor with PPD, a division of thermofisher, in June 2023, where his responsibilities include conducting GcLP compliance audits at clinical trial facilities around the world.
