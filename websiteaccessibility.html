Accessibility Statement.

We are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. We believe everyone should be able to access our content, services, and learning opportunities with ease and dignity.


Our Commitment.

We strive to make our website user-friendly and inclusive by following best practices and recognized accessibility standards, including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).


Accessibility Features.

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

a) Keyboard navigation support.
b) Alternative text for images where applicable
c) Descriptive links and headings for easier navigation.
d) Responsive design for different devices and screen sizes.


Ongoing Improvements.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our website to identify and fix accessibility issues and improve usability for all visitors.


Known Limitations.

While we aim to make all content accessible, some older content or third-party tools may not fully meet accessibility standards. We are actively working to address these areas.


Feedback and Support.

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on our website or have suggestions on how we can improve accessibility, please contact us. We value your feedback and are committed to making improvements.
